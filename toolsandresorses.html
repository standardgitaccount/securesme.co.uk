Free tools that are usefull to maintain privacy, security, and most importantly, data protection.

Privacy International Framework

Privacy International (PI) is a charity based in the UK and was formed in 1990. PI has hundrads of helpfull reports for SMEs on diverse topics and also publish a weekley newsletter.

Visit the PI website




Cyber Essentials Framework

The Cyber Essentials framework is run by the National Cyber Security Center and provides certifecation to an organisation that meets the minimum cyber security standards. This also requires yearly audits to maintain the certificate.

Visit the Cyber Essentials website
